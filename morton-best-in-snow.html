In 2012, an ASPCA donation drive had thousands of new pet-crazy fans joining the Morton Salt Facebook page. But with little maintenance done to keep them interested and Facebook's rules changing every day, something needed to be done to reactivate them in 2013.

 

So we created the Morton Best in Snow Pet Show. Using Morton's Pet Safe Icemelt - Safe-T-Pet - as the product sponsor behind the Competition, we created a pet show with a difference. Instead of rewarding the best in breed, this Pet Show would reward the creative and downright cute.

 

Every year, winter in North America sees dogs and cats cooped up indoors, unable to enjoy the outdoors. The pet show was created to increase owner awareness of winter protection, while having some fun with their furry friends and helping them get out. And hey, the $10,000 cash prize and awesome pet hampers didn't hurt either.

 

If babies take up the number 1 spot on Facebooks most wall-spammed, then cute pets has to come a close second. And it did. With a total reach of over 3,800,000 people, 2000 pet entries and 50,000 votes from their friends, the Morton Best in Snow Pet Show gave the Morton Salt Pet fans an excuse to share their furry friend with the world. The competition was also listed on various blogs and news channels, as well as being noted by AgencySpy, The Chicago Egotist and Little Black Book.

 

The opening video, above, launched the competition to our fans.

The app lived on Morton Salt's Facebook page and allowed you to upload and share entries with friends. The more friends that voted for your pet, the better your chances were at winning that $10,000. While inside the app, you could also browse other entries and learn about Safe-T-Pet Ice Melt and it's donations to the ASPCA.

 

A large part of the world accesses Facebook on the move, not to mention that it's also when they're closest to their camera. We created a fully functional mobile app that allowed you to access the page, submit and even vote, remotely.
