The Posters: (Click for higher resolution)

In 1893 the World's Fair came to Chicago. It was a spectacle the likes of which the World had never seen before - The White City as it was called, became the show that put Chicago on the map.

 

The Field Museum was launching their exhibition of The World's Fair of 1893 and the challenge was to get people excited about it. What better way to do this than to let our audience view the World's Fair through the eyes of people back then? We created a time machine.

The app was downloadable from the posters via NFC or QR code:

The Augmented Reality tour around them extended the scene from each poster.
