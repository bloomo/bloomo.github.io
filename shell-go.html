In 2003 Shell desperately needed to change their brand image in South Africa. As far as the consumer was concerned, Shell had lost touch with the everyman. It made fuel for F1 cars and motorheads. We decided it was time to re-awaken a classic ad campaign of the past, "Go well Go Shell". It's a phrase that is still used in South Africa today.
